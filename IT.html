Department of Information Technology(IT)

What is The Study of Information Technology 

Information technology (IT) is the use of any computers, 
storage, networking and other physical devices, 
infrastructure and processes to create, process, store, 
secure and exchange all forms of electronic data. 
Typically, IT is used in the context of enterprise 
operations as opposed to personal or entertainment 
technologies. The commercial use of IT encompasses
 both computer technology and telephony.

Why Study Information Technology 

Business rely on information technology to help them 
be more productive. This is a career that benefits
 any business by allowing companies to work more 
efficiently and to maximise productivity. And 
with that comes faster communication, electronic
 storage and the protection of important documentation.
